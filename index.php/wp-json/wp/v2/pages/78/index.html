The Apprenticeship Program<\/h2>\n\n\n\n

Apprentices must have completed the Advanced Class and submitted an application along with resume.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

Intern Mechanic \u2013 Ages 16 and under. Work on GS Andiamo Fleet Bicycles and receive volunteer credit.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

Apprentice Mechanic \u2013 Ages 16 and up. Work on GS Andiamo Fleet Bicycles. Paid commensurate with experience.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

Mechanic \u2013 Ages 16 and up. Work on GS Andiamo Fleet and pay for service client Bicycles. May serve as an assistant instructor. Paid commensurate with experience.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

Service Manager \u2013 Ages 18 and up. Oversee mechanics and studio workflow. May serve as an assistant instructor or instructor. Paid commensurate with experience.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

Instructor \u2013 Ages 18 and up. Provides instruction for classes. Paid commensurate with experience.<\/p>\n\n\n\n
