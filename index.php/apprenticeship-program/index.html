The Apprenticeship Program

Apprentices must have completed the Advanced Class and submitted an application along with resume.

Intern Mechanic – Ages 16 and under. Work on GS Andiamo Fleet Bicycles and receive volunteer credit.

Apprentice Mechanic – Ages 16 and up. Work on GS Andiamo Fleet Bicycles. Paid commensurate with experience.

Mechanic – Ages 16 and up. Work on GS Andiamo Fleet and pay for service client Bicycles. May serve as an assistant instructor. Paid commensurate with experience.

Service Manager – Ages 18 and up. Oversee mechanics and studio workflow. May serve as an assistant instructor or instructor. Paid commensurate with experience.

Instructor – Ages 18 and up. Provides instruction for classes. Paid commensurate with experience.
